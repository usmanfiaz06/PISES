1. Overview & Getting Started

The PISES Revenue & Cost Projection Simulator is a single-file, browser-based financial model that projects enrollment, revenue, staffing, and profitability for the PISES new campus over a 7-year horizon (Year 0 = Current through Year 6).

How to open: Simply double-click PISES_Revenue_Simulator.html in any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari). No installation, internet, or server needed.

How it works: Every slider or number input you change instantly recalculates all projections in real-time. There is no "Submit" or "Calculate" button — the model is always live.

Tip: All monetary values are in Saudi Riyals (SAR). Reference: 1 USD = 3.75 SAR.

3.5 STAFF COSTS

Monthly salary inputs for each staff category. The model calculates annual cost as: headcount × monthly salary × 12 months × benefits multiplier.

Benefits MultiplierMultiplied on top of salary to account for housing allowance, GOSI, end-of-service, medical insurance1.00× – 1.50×1.25×1.20× – 1.35× typical

How Staff Headcount Is Calculated

  • Teachers = Total sections × teacher ratio per segment (EY: 1.0, PRI: 1.15, INT: 1.35, SEC: 1.45 teachers per section)
  • Senior / HODs = Total sections ÷ 10 (1 coordinator per ~10 sections)
  • Admin = Total students ÷ 100 (1 admin per ~100 students)
  • Support = Total students ÷ 150 (1 support staff per ~150 students)
Note: The teacher ratio is higher for older grades because subjects are more specialized (e.g., INT/SEC need separate science, math, language, elective teachers per section).

Breaks down all revenue streams across 7 years:

  • Other Revenue — Books, Registration, Transport, Uniform, Sports, Cafeteria

7. Worked Example — Quick Start

Follow these steps to model a common scenario:

Scenario: "What if we target 7,000 students over 5 years with a 10% discount for 3 years?"

  1. Open the simulator in your browser — defaults already match this scenario.
  2. Check the KPI bar at the top center — note the Annual Revenue, Costs, and Net Surplus for Y5.
  3. Click the P&L tab — check the Annual Surplus row. Are all years positive?
  4. Look at the Health Check (right panel) — is Staff Cost % green (<60%)? Is Net Margin strong?
  5. Click Y1 in the year selector — this is the first year with the discount. Check if the school is still profitable.

Scenario: "What if we increase fees by 15%?"

  1. Go to the Tuition Fees card (gold header).
  2. For each grade, change the Fee field. E.g., Nursery from 6,000 to 6,900, Reception from 7,000 to 8,050, etc.
  3. Watch the KPI bar and mini chart update in real-time.
  4. Check the KSA Range column — are your new fees still within the benchmark range?

Scenario: "What if teacher salaries increase to SAR 7,000?"

  1. Go to the Staff Costs card (red header).
  2. Drag the Teacher Salary slider to 7,000.
  3. Watch the benchmark hint below — it should show a green dot (still within KSA range).
  4. Check the P&L tab — how does Net Margin change?
  5. Check the Health Check — has Staff Cost % moved from green to orange?

Scenario: "What if enrollment grows slower — 8 years instead of 5?"

  1. Go to the Growth Trajectory card.
  2. Drag Growth Period slider from 5 to 8.
  3. The KPI bar now shows Y8 values (or Y6 if capped). Enrollment grows more gradually.
  4. Check the mini chart — the revenue-cost gap should narrow in early years.
  5. Click Staffing tab — new hires per year should be smaller (spread over more years).
